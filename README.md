# Chocolate sales analysis dashboard
## Project Overview

This Business Intelligence project analyses chocolate sales data to uncover revenue trends, product performance, regional contribution, and salesperson effectiveness.

The dashboard was designed to simulate a real-world business scenario where stakeholders require a clear, interactive, and insight-driven sales performance report.

## Business Problem
- Management needs visibility into:
- Overall revenue performance
- Monthly and quarterly sales trends
- Top and underperforming products
- Regional sales distribution
- Individual salesperson contribution
The goal is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights using Power BI.

## Dashboard Overview
The report includes:
- KPI Cards – Total Sales overview
- Time-Series Analysis – Monthly trend breakdown
- Product Performance Ranking – Revenue by product
- Country-Level Distribution – Revenue contribution by region
- Salesperson Performance Table – Individual comparison
- Interactive Date Slicer – Dynamic filtering

## Key Insights Generated
- A small group of products contributes a significant portion of total revenue.
- Sales performance varies across countries, with certain markets dominating.
- Revenue shows periodic fluctuations indicating possible seasonality.
- Performance differences among sales representatives highlight opportunity areas.

## Dataset Features
- Sales Amount
- Date (Year, Quarter, Month)
- Product Name
- Country
- Sales Person
- Order Status
